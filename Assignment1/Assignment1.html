Creating, compiling and training the model¶

Now we can determine the structure of the neural network model. We have input layer two dense layers.

After this we compile the model. We use adam optimizer with learining rate of 0.0005, loss is measured categorical_crossentropy and we want to see accuracy metrics as well.

After this we start training the model. We train it 1000 times in batches of 16. It is also shuffled between trainings and it also validated with 20% of the data.

In [60]:
model = Sequential([
    Input(shape=(4,)),
    Dense(48, activation='relu'),
    Dense(3, activation='softmax')
])

optimizer = K.optimizers.Adam(learning_rate=0.0005)

model.compile(optimizer=optimizer, loss='categorical_crossentropy', metrics=['accuracy'])

model.fit(X, y, epochs=1000, batch_size=16, verbose=1, shuffle=True, validation_split=0.2)
